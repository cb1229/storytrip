## How It Works

### Phase 1: Story Selection

Users choose from curated narrative templates or create custom stories:

**Pre-Built Narratives:**

| Narrative | Description | Ideal For |
|-----------|-------------|-----------|
| **Underdog Redemption** | Overcoming obstacles, proving doubters wrong, triumph against odds | Fans of struggling teams visiting powerhouses |
| **Rivalry Conquest** | Invading enemy territory, planting your flag, victory in hostile land | Classic rivalry matchups |
| **Family Legacy** | Connecting generations, honoring traditions, passing the torch | Multi-generational trips |
| **The Origin Story** | Discovering roots, understanding history, becoming a "true" fan | First-time visitors to team's city |
| **Brotherhood/Sisterhood** | Friendship, loyalty, shared experiences that bond for life | Friend group trips |
| **The Comeback** | Returning after time away, rediscovering passion, renewal | Fans reconnecting with their team |
| **Bucket List Quest** | Epic achievement, crossing off dreams, life goals | Stadium tours, milestone games |
| **The Long Shot** | Unexpected adventure, spontaneous decisions, happy accidents | Last-minute trips |

**Custom Narratives:**
Users describe their story in their own words, and AI interprets the emotional arc they want to experience.

### Phase 4: The Documentary (Post-Trip)

Within 48-72 hours of returning home, users receive a professionally edited documentary film.

**What the AI Does:**

1. **Ingests all content**: Videos, photos, audio journals, GPS data, timestamps
2. **Identifies narrative beats**: Matches content to the story arc promised
3. **Selects best footage**: AI quality scoring for lighting, stability, emotion
4. **Writes narration**: Generates voiceover script from audio journals
5. **Adds music**: Licensed soundtrack matching emotional beats
6. **Edits to story structure**: Opening, rising action, climax, resolution
7. **Adds graphics**: Title cards, location stamps, score graphics
8. **Produces multiple formats**: Full documentary (15-20 min), social cuts (60-90 sec), highlight reel (3-5 min)

## Technology Stack

### AI Components Required

| Component | Purpose | Approach |
|-----------|---------|----------|
| **Narrative Engine** | Match user intent to story structures | LLM fine-tuned on storytelling frameworks |
| **Itinerary Builder** | Create trips that serve narrative | Constraint optimization + travel APIs |
| **Content Curator** | Select best user footage | Computer vision for quality, emotion detection |
| **Script Writer** | Generate narration from audio journals | Speech-to-text + LLM summarization |
| **Video Editor** | Assemble final documentary | Automated editing pipeline (cuts, transitions, timing) |
| **Music Matcher** | Select appropriate soundtrack | Audio analysis + mood mapping |
| **Voice Synthesis** | Optional professional narration | Text-to-speech or voice cloning (user's voice) |

### Integrations Needed

- **Travel APIs**: Amadeus, Skyscanner, Booking.com, Expedia
- **Maps**: Google Maps, Mapbox for location tracking

- **Sports Data**: ESPN, official league APIs for scores/schedules
- **Music Licensing**: Epidemic Sound, Artlist, or similar
- **Cloud Video**: AWS MediaConvert, Google Video AI
- **Storage**: S3 or equivalent for user content

## Narrative Templates Deep Dive

### Example: "Family Legacy" Template

**Story Arc:**
```
ACT I: THE HERITAGE
- Visit to childhood home / original stadium
- Sharing old stories, photos, memories
- "This is where grandpa used to sit"

ACT II: THE PRESENT
- Current game experience together
- New memories being made
- Bridging past and present

ACT III: THE FUTURE  
- Passing something down (jersey, tradition, seat location)
- Commitment to continue the legacy
- "Someday you'll bring YOUR kids here"
```

**Unique Shot Suggestions:**
- Side-by-side comparison: old photo vs. new photo at same location
- Three generations in frame together
- Hands passing an object (ticket, jersey, etc.)
- Elder teaching younger a chant or tradition

**Unique Audio Prompts:**
- "Tell the story of your first game"
- "What does this team mean to your family?"
- "What do you want them to remember about today?"

**Documentary Style:**
- Warmer color grading
- Nostalgic music selection
- More still photos mixed with video
- Slower pacing, longer moments

## Challenges & Mitigations

### Challenge: Users Won't Capture Enough Content
**Risk:** Documentary requires footage; users might forget or feel awkward recording.

**Mitigations:**
- Smart notifications at optimal moments
- Make capture feel like part of the experience, not interruption
- Gamification: "85% capture completionâ€”finish strong!"
- Minimum viable documentary even with limited footage
- Option to supplement with stock footage of locations

### Challenge: AI Quality Won't Match Professional
**Risk:** Documentaries feel amateur or generic.

**Mitigations:**
- Start with hybrid human+AI editing
- Build quality feedback loops from user ratings
- Offer premium tier with human review
- Under-promise, over-deliver on expectations
- Continuously improve with more training data

### Challenge: Travel Booking is Commoditized
**Risk:** Users might plan through StoryTrip but book elsewhere cheaper.

**Mitigations:**
- Price matching or best-price guarantees
- Documentary quality tied to booking (better data integration)
- Exclusive experiences only available through StoryTrip
- Subscription model reduces per-trip price sensitivity

### Challenge: Technical Complexity
**Risk:** Integrating travel APIs, AI video editing, mobile capture is hard.

**Mitigations:**
- Phase developmentâ€”MVP doesn't need everything
- Partner vs. build where possible
- Hire specifically for AI video expertise
- Accept manual processes early, automate as you scale
